About Company

Software Development

Dexoc is a leading custom Software Development Company based in India that provides enterprise application solution, web application, IT consulting & mobile app development services to clients across the globe. We offer extensive range of innovative software development services to our customers using Microsoft .net, Open source, Blockchain, Native & hybrid mobile application, front-end technology. We ensure your success by delivering business specific solution as every organization has a unique requirement.

Our offshore team is equipped with certified and professionally trained software engineers having vast experience in different business verticals to successfully develop and implement enterprise level IT projects. Our proven methodologies and resources with business domain expertise offer top quality services that add value to your organization.

Our strength lies in the blend of highly technical resources and focused operation with team work environment. We believe in high quality of work ethics and on-schedule delivery with cost effective solution that leads you to the edge of success.
